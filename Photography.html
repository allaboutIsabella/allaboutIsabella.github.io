Inspiration

My love of photography started with inspiration. I always kept seeing these beautiful pictures online of landmarks, the ocean and animals. Then sometimes when I would travel around with my family, I would see these people with tripods standing by cliffs or infront of signs and just taking pictures. So I said to myself, I want to be one of those people who have their pictures online, I want to be able to be the person whos pictues bring light into others, but there was only one problem...I didn't have a camera. I asked my dad if we could get an actual camera and my wish came true. Today whenever we travel of just go outside I always have my camera. The more pictures I take, the more I impress my family and bring a smile to their faces.
